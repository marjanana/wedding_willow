Willow Paule documented Kopernik's first technology agent training with PEKKA (women’s group] in Lombok, Indonesia and we couldn't have gotten a better person to do it. In all her photos she captures more than just what the eye can see—the energy and hearts of the women involved really shine through in the end products. This is more than what we wanted, as the event was not about us but more about the special PEKKA women who attended the training. Willow is also a terrific person to work with and always so willing to help beyond her duties. We're glad to have worked with her and hope to see her again in more of our events!"

- Cindy Nawilis, Kopernik
